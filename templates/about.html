Our Story

From One Bucket to Thousands of Homes

Founded in 2015 by Maria & James Fresher, Freshious Cleaning started as a small family operation with a big dream — to make professional, affordable cleaning accessible to everyone.

Over a decade later, we've grown into a trusted team of 50+ cleaners serving thousands of homes and businesses across the region. Our secret? We treat every client's space as if it were our own.
